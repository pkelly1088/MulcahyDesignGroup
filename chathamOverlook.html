Mill Pond Overlook

Chatham

Tom was a pleasure to work with and did a fantastic job masterminding and spearheading the landscape planning and design for our new home. From our very first interaction, Tom was attentive and intuitive and worked seamlessly to iterate with us to land on the perfect site design and planting plan for our home. Undaunted by a tricky hillside location with a host of material grade changes and lot size constraints, Tom engineered a plan that blends seamlessly into the surroundings and looks like it's been there for years. His extensive knowledge of local plants and horticulture allowed a planting plan with thoughtful details for all seasons, and the finished product is truly spectacular and the subject of countless compliments from our neighbors and passersby. We couldn’t be happier with Tom’s partnership, professionalism and vision and look forward to working with him again.

-S.C.

Project completed while with another landscape architecture firm.
