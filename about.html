Mulcahy Design Group

Thomas Mulcahy, President

Founded in 2020 Mulcahy Design Group is a Landscape Design firm servicing the Greater Boston & Cape Cod areas. Focusing on residential landscapes. Thomas Mulcahy brings over 12 years of professional experience as a Landscape Designer to the firm. Thomas studied Landscape Architecture at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. Thomas has extensive experience in conceptual design, project management, construction detailing & construction administration. He has served as a Project Manager for a number of prominent national & international projects ranging from Resort, Mixed-Use Development, Institutional, Academic & Corporate, Multi-family Residential & High End Residential. He is a current member of the Association of Professional Landscape Designers.
